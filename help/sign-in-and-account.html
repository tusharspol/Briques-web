Sign in to Briques

← All help articles

Briques uses Sign in with Google and, on iOS, Sign in with Apple. There's no separate password to manage — you sign in with an account you already have.

Signing in

  1. Open Briques and choose Continue with Google or Continue with Apple.
  2. Approve the sign-in. You're in — your apps and data are tied to that account.

Switching devices

Sign in with the same account on a new phone and your apps come with you. On Pro, your records sync through the cloud; on Free, data stays on the device where you created it.

Sign in with Apple and private email: If you used Apple's "Hide My Email," your account shows a private relay address. That's expected and works normally — just use Sign in with Apple again on any new device.

Trouble signing in?

See troubleshooting, or email support@briques.app.
