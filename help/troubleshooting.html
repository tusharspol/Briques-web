Troubleshooting

← All help articles

Most issues clear up with one of the steps below. If yours doesn't, contact support — we read everything.

My app didn't generate

  • Check your connection and try again — generation needs to reach our servers.
  • Make sure you have enough AI credits. If you're out, top up or wait for the monthly refresh.
  • If a prompt was blocked, rephrase it. Briques won't generate disallowed content.
  • Try a shorter, more specific description, then build on it once the app exists.

I can't sign in

  • Use the same method you signed up with — Google, or Apple on iOS.
  • Update to the latest version of the app.
  • If you used Apple's "Hide My Email," use Sign in with Apple again rather than typing an address.

My data isn't showing on a new device

  • Sign in with the same account.
  • On Free, data stays on the device where you created it; cloud sync is a Pro feature.

I paid but don't see Pro

Tap Restore purchases in Plans, signed in to the store account you bought with. See restore purchases.

Still need help?

Can't find what you need? Email us and a human replies within about two days.
