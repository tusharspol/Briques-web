What's a credit?

A credit is the unit of AI work. Generating an app costs ~40 credits on the Fast model. Editing one screen, ~10. A small chat tweak, ~3. Every action shows an estimate before it runs and the actual cost after.

Top up anytime

Run out mid-build? One-time packs from $2.99. Pack credits never expire and stay yours through cancellation, downgrade, or anything else.

No surprise bills

Credits cap your AI spend at the plan's allowance. Storage and bandwidth aren't separately billable. The monthly fee is the monthly fee.

Pro subscriptions are purchased and managed inside the app, via Apple on iOS, Google on Android. Pricing on this page is for reference. Refunds for system failures are automatic. See terms for the full mechanics.
